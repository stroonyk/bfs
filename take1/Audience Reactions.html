Audience Reactions

After each film screened, we invite members of the audience for their feedback. We analyse this feedback and calculate an "audience reaction score" for each film. This feedback provides us with valuable information on how well the committee are doing with film selection.

These are the scores awarded by the audience on the Reaction Sheets after each showing

The overall rating for each film is calculated by applying the following weightings to the audience's responses: Excellent - 1; Good - 0.75; Fair - 0.5; Poor - 0.25; and Awful - 0. The sum of the weighted scores is then expressed as a percentage of the total number of responses.
